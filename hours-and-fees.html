Hours & Fees

My office hours are Sunday through Thursday by appointment. Therapy Sessions are typically 60-90 minutes in length. Most clients come weekly.

I accept cash or checks. I'm sorry I presently do not accept insurance. I am happy to create a Super Bill for you to give to your insurance company and will complete the necessary paperwork for Victim Witness funding. Please check with your insurance company a head of time for information about your reimbursement options.

I provide a limited number of low cost counseling options based on your ability to pay. Please feel free to talk to me about these options. All information is confidential. I will try to work with you to make services accessible within your budget.
