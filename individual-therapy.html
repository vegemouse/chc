Individual Therapy

As a Marriage and Family Therapist I work with individuals in the context of relationships. These relationships can be with a significant other, parent or family member, a colleague or peer. As we move through various transition periods of our lives we are faced with decisions that can sometimes prove challenging. I can help you through these challenges. I work with clients at all phases of the life cycle and who are trying to cope with a variety of life’s challenges.

  • Moving away from home for the first time
  • Considering a new relationship or ending a present one
  • LGBTQ issues, including considering coming out, coming to terms with and/or supporting a loved one
  • Adult Adoptee Issues
  • Birth Parent Relinquishment Decisions
  • Caring for an elderly parent
  • Raising a special need child
